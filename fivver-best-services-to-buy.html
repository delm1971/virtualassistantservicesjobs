Best Practices for Vetting Sellers Effectively

To bypass friction and establish seamless partnerships from your very first order, follow these three operational rules:

  • Focus on Level Badges: Prioritize sellers possessing "Level 2," "Top Rated," or "Fiverr's Choice" status. These designations signal clear histories of meeting delivery timelines, maintaining high review scores, and keeping response rates swift.
  • Inspect Real Portfolio Samples: Click into the seller's primary gig card and scroll through the carousel to see live samples of completed work delivered to previous clients, rather than relying solely on the cover graphic.
  • Communicate Before Ordering: Send a quick direct message explaining your project parameters before placing an order. This aligns expectations, ensures the freelancer has immediate availability, and verifies their technical grasp of your task.
